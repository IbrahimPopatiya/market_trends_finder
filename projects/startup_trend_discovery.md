# 🧠 Startup Trend Discovery Workflow

## Goal
Discover high-performing startups in the US that are trending on social media and analyze their core business model.

## Steps
1.  **Scrape TikTok Trends**: Find viral products or service mentions.
2.  **Analyze Growth Signals**: Check social media engagement and web traffic.
3.  **Identify Business Model**: Subscription, e-commerce, SaaS, etc.
4.  **Save Results**: Log findings into `results.json`.

## Utility Script Generation
Generating a specialized scraper `social_trends.py` to automate step 1.
