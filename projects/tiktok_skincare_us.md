# 🧴 TikTok Skincare US Discovery Workflow

## Goal
Identify viral US skincare products, ingredients, or routines that are gaining massive traction and analyze their business model for India replication.

## Steps
1.  **Direct Scraping**: Search "#skincaretrends" and "#skincareus" on TikTok.
2.  **Viral Analysis**: Filter for products with >1M views in the last 30 days.
3.  **Business Intelligence**:
    - Identify the Brand & Core Product.
    - Extract the "Viral Hook" (e.g., "The Slugging Method").
    - Analyze the Monetization (D2C vs Marketplace).
4.  **AI Reconstruction**: Use OpenAI to generate a replication strategy for the Indian market.

## Tool Generation
- **`tiktok_skincare_scraper.py`**: Specialized scraper for skincare hashtags.
