# 📈 Finance & News Trend Analysis Workflow

## Goal
Extract high-level business and finance market signals from Newsletters (e.g. Trends.vc, Milk Road), Forbes, and B2B Marketplaces.

## Steps
1.  **News Extraction**: Pull data from "Forbes Tech" and "Startup News" RSS feeds.
2.  **Newsletter Parsing**: Analyze trends from "B2B SaaS Gaps" newsletters.
3.  **Finance Signal Detection**:
    - Identify industries with >$10M funding in the last 6 months.
    - Find "Emerging Markets" in APAC or India where these sectors are currently undersized.
4.  **Strategic Analysis**:
    - Identify Monetization Gaps.
    - Create a "Go-to-Market" playbook for India.

## Tool Generation
- **`finance_news_analyzer.py`**: Specialized tools for news scraping.
